SUPERPAPERS

AI-Powered Academic Research From Idea to Published Paper

The Problem

AI Alone Is Not Enough

Raw chat is useful, but serious research needs a structured way to think with AI.

01

No Shared Research Process

AI can suggest many paths, but there is no disciplined way to debate trade-offs and converge on one design.

02

No Durable Consensus

Good ideas vanish across prompts when assumptions, decisions, and rejected alternatives are not documented.

03

No Reproducible Record

Without a structured pipeline, AI-assisted research turns into scattered chat instead of a traceable paper workflow.

Superpapers turns AI into a research partner.

What It Is

A Research Plugin for Claude Code

Inspired by Superpowers. Adapted to empirical quantitative research.

Superpapers gives researchers a structured way to think with Claude, converge on a design, and keep the full workflow documented and reproducible.

Brainstorm → Plan → Execute → Submit

Built Around 4 Stages

Brainstorm. Plan. Execute. Submit.

Each stage leaves artifacts behind: design choices, execution plans, scripts, outputs, and a reproducible paper trail.
